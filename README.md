# PLCIS
**ProjectLink Construction Intelligence System**  
  
  _An integrated system to manage the construction information._


**Tutorial:**
1. Go to ribbon "New Report"
2. Fill in the respective information for the project. Turn on the GPS to record the GPS coordinates data.
3. Fill in the information for the team members.
4. Select the amount of layouts required for site plan, then click Apply. (Max. 10 site plan for PLCISv9)
5. Upload all the required plan. (Only applicable for one page of plan per layout)
6. Take photos / Upload the site photos. (The generated GPS coordinates is based on the report GPS coordinates data)
7. Double click the site photo for the details of the photo.
8. Pin photo onto the site plan - Go to the ribbon "Photos", single click the photo required, go to ribbon "Site Plan", find the repestive plan and pin the location for the respective photo.
9. Successful pinned photo will be shown under the plan layout. Pinned photo can be easily removed by using the delete option.
10. Go to ribbon "New Report", click Save Site Report, click "Preview A4 Layout" to generate the report, then save/print out the report.
11. Complete!
